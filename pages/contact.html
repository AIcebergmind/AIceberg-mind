How to Reach Us

We prefer thoughtful, detailed communications over brief messages. When reaching out, please share:

  • Your Background: Professional context and relevant experience
  • Collaboration Interest: Specific areas where you see potential synergy
  • Vision Alignment: How your values connect with our mission
  • Proposed Next Steps: What kind of partnership or collaboration you envision

Email: We'll provide direct contact information to aligned partners and collaborators. For now, use the contact form on our main page to begin the conversation.
